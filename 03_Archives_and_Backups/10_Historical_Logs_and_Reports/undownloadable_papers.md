This report provides a systematic inventory and detailed categorization of all currently missing PDFs from the **Boundary Spanning Meta-Analysis** dataset, specifically formatted to facilitate final manual retrieval.

## 📌 Executive Summary

Out of the total dataset of 732 articles, exactly **106 articles (14.48%)** remain undownloaded. To maximize retrieval efficiency, these 106 items are classified into two distinct types based on the presence of a Digital Object Identifier (DOI):

1. **Type 1: Zotero Target Papers (DOI Present) — 57 articles**
   * These papers possess valid DOIs but belong to publishers with strict anti-scraping walls (e.g., Academy of Management, JSTOR, INFORMS).
   * **Recommendation:** Import via DOI into Zotero and use the *'Find Available PDFs'* function, or manually access through UCL Library Search.
2. **Type 2: Ghost Papers (DOI Missing) — 49 articles**
   * These papers lack a formal DOI entirely. Many are older proceedings, dissertations, or fragmented citations.
   * **Recommendation:** Copy the paper title and manually search directly in **Google Scholar** or **ResearchGate**.
