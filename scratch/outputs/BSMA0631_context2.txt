We chose the manufacturing industry as the research  sample because it plays a predominant role in driving China's  economic advancement and has the highest carbon dioxide  (CO2) emissions among industrial sectors (Zhao et al. 2017).  Hence, under China's climate crisis response policies, there  exists an urgent necessity for SPI within the manufacturing in- dustry to facilitate the transition from extensive growth to sus- tainable development. We developed an English language questionnaire based on  existing literature and enlisted two independent translators  to translate the questionnaire into Chinese language and per- form back translation. In total, 321 questionnaires were returned, and after rigorous  screening, 85 invalid questionnaires were excluded, resulting  in a total of 236 responses received. The exclusion of invalid  questionnaires was due to data missing content and obvious  errors in common sense content.
